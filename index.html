Art Styles

Portraiture

A portrait is typically defined as a representation of a specific individual, such as the artist might meet in life. A portrait does not merely record someone’s features, however, but says something about who he or she is, offering a vivid sense of a real person’s presence. Read more here.

Concept

The main goal of concept art is to convey a visual representation of a design, idea, and/or mood for use in films, video games, animation, or comic books before it is put into the final product. Read more here.

WPAP

WPAP (Wedha Pop Art Portrait) is human portrait illustration style (usually famous figures) dominated flat fields bloom color is placed in the front, middle, and back to cause dimension. Read more here.

Art and Coding

I am a 19 years old college student from the Philippines and I am currently in my 3rd year, studying Computer Science in the Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila. I started working as a freelance illustrator to support the finances in my university. I have done several graphic designs before like: brand poster, branding identity, t-shirt designs, portraiture and concept art. I never fail to become diligent with every art piece that I did and I also value deadlines. As a home-based employee, I am always responsive to the client's inquiries and I also value their trust. I also have done several programs throughout my academic years as we learned the programming languages, C, C++, COBOL, Java and PHP.

I make videos!

Most of the videos that I make and edit are only 6-7 seconds long. I appreciate the challenge in compressing everything in a little time and I think it brings out more of my creativity!

The tools that I use in creating and editing videos are: Canon 1300D, 50 mm F1.8 lens, Canon Kit lens, Sony Vegas Pro and Adobe After Effects. I give full credit to the artists on Soundcloud and Youtube for the music that I used.
